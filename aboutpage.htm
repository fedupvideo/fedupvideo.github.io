    This is fedupvideo *enough, a small youtube channel I made where I post the songs I made and gaming content to entertain others and tutorials to help others do some of the things I do :D

 

    My songs are produced with Image-Line FL Studio 9, I mostly use what they have in there and sometimes use other free samples I could find on the internet to help make my songs sound more unique and more fun to listen to

    Videos and graphics I used are made with Windows Movie Maker (don't have money to buy more complicated editors :P) and with Paint

    I also sometimes use alternatives like Paint.NET and Flash from time to time when I feel like I want to make something cool :)

 

---------------

    fedupvideo *enough is made by 4 members from the OMFG community to recreate the original channel that follows similar creativity and artistic approach to continue the uniqueness that was unfortunately short-lived and never took off, pretty much was create so we can experience another fedupvideo era :)

    The channel will also branch out to other types of content like gaming and short animations to add more variety and might also help bringing in new audience unfamiliar to OMFG and fedupvideo and help rebuild a whole mini-community around it

    Also note that this channel is not run by the original Loell Bergen/OMFG, we only want to continue the style of the old stuff he makes. Due to that, we will be using the name "Loell Burger" instead xD
